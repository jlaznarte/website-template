On the identifiability of TSK additive fuzzy rule-based models

Aznarte, J.L., Benítez, J.M.

Department of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, University of Granada, Granada, Spain

Abstract

Set Theory has been developed during the second half of last century, with a starting point in L.A. Zadeh seminal paper [14]. From that moment on, there has been a harsh debate between scientifics supporting it and others believing that it was an unnecesary mathematical construct, generally opposing it to Probability Theory. Those researchers usually complained about the alleged lack of mathematical soundness of Fuzzy Logic and its applications. For a succint review on this debate, see Section 1 of [2]. © 2006 Springer.
